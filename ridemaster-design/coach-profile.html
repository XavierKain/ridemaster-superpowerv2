About Maria

With over 12 years of experience in kitesurfing and 5 years in wingfoil, I've dedicated my life to helping students discover the thrill of wind-powered water sports. My journey started right here in Tarifa, where the legendary Levante winds first introduced me to kiting at age 16.

After competing professionally for 6 years and traveling to over 30 countries chasing the best conditions, I found my true passion in coaching. There's nothing more rewarding than seeing a student's face light up when they complete their first water start or land their first jump. I specialize in taking beginners from zero to independent riders, as well as helping intermediate kiters unlock their freestyle potential.

My teaching philosophy centers on building confidence through progressive skill development. Every student learns differently, so I adapt my approach to match your learning style, goals, and comfort level. Safety is always my top priority - the ocean deserves respect, and I make sure every session is both fun and secure.
