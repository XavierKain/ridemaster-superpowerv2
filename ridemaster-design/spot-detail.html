About Tarifa

Known as the wind capital of Europe, Tarifa sits at the southernmost point of continental Europe where the Atlantic Ocean meets the Mediterranean Sea. This unique geographical position creates the legendary Levante and Poniente winds that blow consistently throughout the year, making it one of the world's premier kitesurfing destinations.

The town itself is a charming blend of Moorish architecture, whitewashed buildings, and vibrant surf culture. With miles of pristine sandy beaches, crystal-clear waters, and a laid-back atmosphere, Tarifa offers the perfect combination of world-class riding conditions and authentic Spanish charm.

Whether you're a complete beginner looking to catch your first winds or an advanced rider seeking to perfect your freestyle moves, Tarifa has something for everyone. The variety of beaches caters to all skill levels, from the flat-water lagoon spots perfect for learning to the wave-riding paradise of Valdevaqueros.

Best Time to Visit

Tarifa enjoys consistent wind year-round, but the peak season runs from April to October when the Levante wind is most reliable. Summer months (June-August) offer the strongest and most consistent conditions, ideal for experienced riders. Spring and autumn provide excellent conditions with fewer crowds, perfect for beginners and intermediate riders.
