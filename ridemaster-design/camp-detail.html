About This Camp

Join us for an unforgettable week of kitesurfing in Tarifa, Europe's wind capital. This intermediate-level camp is designed for kiters who have mastered the basics and want to take their riding to the next level. With consistent thermal winds averaging 20-30 knots and the stunning backdrop of the African coastline, Tarifa offers perfect conditions for progression.

Our structured program includes 5 days of intensive coaching, video analysis sessions, and theory workshops. You'll work on upwind riding, transitions, jumps, and board recovery techniques. Small group sizes ensure personalized attention, while our experienced team provides all the support you need to achieve your kiting goals.
